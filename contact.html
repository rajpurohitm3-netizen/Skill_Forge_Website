Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply browse our courses, click on the course you're interested in, and click the "Enroll Now" button. You'll be guided through the payment process and get instant access to the course materials.

Do you offer certificates upon completion?

Yes! All our courses come with industry-recognized certificates upon successful completion. These certificates can be shared on LinkedIn and included in your professional portfolio.

What if I'm not satisfied with a course?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all our courses. If you're not completely satisfied within the first 30 days, we'll provide a full refund, no questions asked.

Do you provide job placement assistance?

Absolutely! Our career services team provides resume reviews, interview preparation, and connects you with our hiring partner network. We have a 95% job placement rate for our graduates.

Can I access courses on mobile devices?

Yes! Our platform is fully responsive and works seamlessly on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile. You can learn anytime, anywhere at your own pace.

How long do I have access to course materials?

Once you enroll in a course, you get lifetime access to all course materials, including any future updates. You can revisit the content whenever you need a refresher.
